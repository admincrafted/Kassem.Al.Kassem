### :mortar_board: 3. StudyBuddy Full-Stack Application
A comprehensive full-stack student productivity application designed to bridge the gap between simple task trackers and complex academic management systems. Built       with React, TypeScript, Express.js, and PostgreSQL, featuring AI-powered study assistance via Google's Gemini API.

  ## 🌟 Overview
  StudyBuddy Planner helps students manage their academic life with:
  - **Task Management** : Organize assignments with priorities, due dates, and status tracking
  - **Weekly Timetable**: Visual schedule for classes and activities
  - **Smart Notes** : AI-powered note summarization and organization
  - **Calendar View** : Monthly overview of tasks and classes
  - **Progress Analytics** : Track completion rates and productivity streaks
  - **AI Assistant** : Personalized study tips and chatbot support
  - **Notifications** : Email reminders and web push notifications
  - **Export Features** : PDF and CSV exports for tasks and timetable
